Project Manager

AWNER - Vedacit

Still at AWNER, I led a high-impact project for Vedacit, focused on creating a robust inventory counting and traceability system, due to the low accuracy and control offered by the existing ERP and WMS. I developed the entire system flowchart, managed the project from end to end, and directly led a developer, in addition to the teams involved in the field. On the day of the count—a critical and chaotic moment within the operation—I was present at the São Paulo factory, actively resolving technical problems in real time. The system, used simultaneously in two other factories, was operated by more than 70 users in real time. The logic included rigorous validations: an operator would label and register a position in the collector, being prevented from counting the same position in the second stage, ensuring traceability and process integrity. The project was an absolute success, allowing Vedacit to go from an annual loss of R$ 7 million to practically zero, thanks to the new level of accuracy and control over inventory, in addition to enabling modular counts in critical production areas.
